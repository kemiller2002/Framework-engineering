# FE-012C Repeatability Run 001 Pre-Registration

## Purpose

Record expectations and failure signals before collecting repeatability-run outputs.

## Predictions

- Entry primitive agreement should remain high.
- Reasoning shape agreement should remain high.
- Exit primitive and dominant primitive may show more drift.
- Transition agreement should remain moderate to strong.
- Recognition bias will likely remain a threat.
- Candidate missing primitives should remain rare if vocabulary is stable.

## Failure Signals

- Same model produces substantially different primitive sequences from the prior run.
- Entry primitive agreement drops sharply.
- Reasoning shape agreement drops sharply.
- Candidate missing primitives increase materially.
- Transition agreement becomes weak across multiple packets.

## Interpretation Boundary

These predictions are provisional expectations for measurement repeatability.

They are not claims that repeatability has already been demonstrated.
