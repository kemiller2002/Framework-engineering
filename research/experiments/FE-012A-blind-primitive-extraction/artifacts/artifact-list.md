## Diversity Rationale

- The set spans strategy, diagnosis, learning, operations, risk, planning, and incident response.
- Some artifacts are cyclic, some classificatory, some comparative, and some intervention-oriented.
- Several artifacts are strongly branded, which makes them useful tests of whether blinding can remove surface identity while preserving reasoning flow.
- The set includes artifacts that are likely to share partial primitives while still differing in domain language and presentation style.

## Exclusions

- Clarity is excluded from the extraction set.
- EDF is excluded from the extraction set.
- Framework Engineering terminology should not be used to derive primitives during the initial extraction phase.
