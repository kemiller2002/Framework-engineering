4. Prioritizing habitat restoration projects
5. Investigating contamination in a pharmaceutical plant
6. Designing an archaeological excavation workflow
7. Responding to misinformation during a public health event
8. Coordinating lunar habitat maintenance
9. Selecting scientific instruments for an interstellar probe
10. Managing restoration of a historic cathedral

## Selection Note

The set is intentionally varied across logistics, culture, robotics, ecology, manufacturing, archaeology, public communication, space operations, scientific selection, and restoration planning.

The goal is not realism perfection.

The goal is to stress whether the primitive vocabulary transfers across unrelated procedural problems.
