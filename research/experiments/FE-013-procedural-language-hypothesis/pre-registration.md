# FE-013 Pre-Registration

## Predictions

- Scientific Method, OODA, Scrum, and Differential Diagnosis will produce richer ASTs than SWOT or Bloom.
- Bloom will produce a shallow or hierarchical AST rather than a procedural control-flow AST.
- SWOT will produce a shallow assessment or classification structure with weak termination logic.
- Cyclic artifacts will expose explicit loop conditions.
- Diagnostic artifacts will expose branch conditions and verification or termination constraints.
- Static artifacts will expose fewer preconditions, loops, and termination conditions.

## Failure Signals

- All six artifacts produce indistinguishable ASTs.
- Models cannot extract ASTs consistently.
- ASTs depend mostly on artifact recognition.
- Static artifacts produce artificial procedural structure.
- Constraint extraction becomes too subjective to compare.
