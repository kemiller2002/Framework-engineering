# SWOT Scenario

A regional restaurant chain is experiencing declining visits despite maintaining positive online reviews.

New competitors have launched delivery-first offers and loyalty programs in the same markets.

The chain has limited capital for major expansion or renovation, but it still has a strong local reputation in several neighborhoods.

Staffing instability has increased schedule disruptions and service inconsistency.

Food costs are rising, and leadership must decide where to focus near-term strategic attention.

Near-term decisions may affect menu strategy, staffing priorities, customer retention efforts, and delivery partnerships.
