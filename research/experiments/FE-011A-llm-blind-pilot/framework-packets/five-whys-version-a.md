# Five Whys Version A

Use the structure below to investigate the scenario.

## Problem Boundary

State the problem being analyzed and what is in scope.

## Observations

Record the observed evidence relevant to the incident.

## Why Chain

Develop a plausible why chain from the observed problem.

## Alternative Hypotheses

List other plausible explanations that should not be ignored.

## Evidence for Each Why

Tie each why statement to available evidence or note missing evidence.

## Confidence

Use categorical confidence only.

## Verification

State what should be checked next to confirm or revise the analysis.
