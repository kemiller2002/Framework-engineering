# SWOT Output 2

## Strengths

- Positive online reviews
- Strong local reputation in several neighborhoods

## Weaknesses

- Declining visits
- Limited capital for major expansion or renovation
- Staffing instability
- Service inconsistency caused by schedule disruption
- Rising food costs

## Opportunities

- Consider adopting delivery-oriented or loyalty-oriented responses because competitors are succeeding with those approaches
- Use the existing local reputation to support customer retention

## Threats

- Competitors with delivery-first offers
- Competitors with loyalty programs
- Continued increases in food costs
- Reputation damage if service inconsistency continues
