# OODA Output 1

## Observe

- The cybersecurity team has detected unusual network traffic.
- The traffic may indicate ransomware activity.
- Telemetry is incomplete.
- There is time pressure.
- Executives want a rapid recommendation.
- The team is considering immediate isolation, additional evidence gathering, or narrower containment.

## Orient

The team is operating under material uncertainty. A full enterprise-wide isolation response may be costly if the signal is a false positive, but waiting too long could increase damage if active compromise is underway.

## Decide

Take a narrow containment-first approach while gathering more evidence in parallel.

## Act

- Isolate the most suspicious segment or hosts first if they can be identified.
- Increase monitoring and collect additional telemetry immediately.
- Prepare for broader isolation if stronger evidence of ransomware activity emerges.

Cycle as needed.
