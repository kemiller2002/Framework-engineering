## Recognition Decay Patterns

### rapid_decay

Recognition disappears once canonical prose is removed.

### gradual_decay

Recognition weakens gradually as cues are removed.

### persistent_recognition

Recognition persists through graph or graph perturbations.

### provider_specific_recognition

One provider recognizes while others do not.

### unstable_recognition

Recognition varies without clear relation to representation changes.
