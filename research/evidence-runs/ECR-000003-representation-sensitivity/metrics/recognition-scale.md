# Recognition Scale

Use four values:

- `recognized`
- `partial`
- `unknown`
- `not_recognized`

## Definitions

### recognized

The model explicitly names or clearly identifies the source framework, procedural family, or canonical reasoning pattern.

### partial

The model does not name the source artifact but identifies a closely related procedural family.

### unknown

The model explicitly says unknown, unclear, or cannot identify.

### not_recognized

The model leaves `recognized_artifact` empty or states that no recognition occurred.
