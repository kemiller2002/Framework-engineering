# Validation Roadmap

## Phase 1

Internal adversarial review.

Purpose:
Stress the theory against its strongest competing explanations before external replication.

## Phase 2

Different-model replication.

Purpose:
Test whether the current results survive model-family changes and prompt variation.

## Phase 3

Human analyst replication.

Purpose:
Test whether the findings survive beyond internal same-model execution.

## Phase 4

Domain expert challenge.

Purpose:
Test whether external experts regard the claims as coherent, distinctive, or unnecessary.

## Phase 5

Publication readiness review.

Purpose:
Decide whether any claims are strong enough to communicate beyond the internal research program.
