# Research

Status: Working draft

Research contains hypotheses, experiments, evidence, corpora, active questions, deferred insights, and failed hypotheses.

Research is exploratory.

Research artifacts should clearly state status:

- Hypothesis
- Under Investigation
- Provisionally Supported
- Foundation Candidate
- Superseded
- Failed
