# Abstraction Governance

## Purpose

Prevent infinite abstraction ladders.

## New Abstraction Test

A proposed abstraction may be introduced only if it satisfies at least two of:

- explains disagreement that lower-level representations cannot explain
- improves prediction
- improves measurement reliability
- reduces complexity
- produces clearer kill conditions
- improves Clarity or EDF product design without acting as product validation

## Examples

- Primitive
- Functional role
- Constraint
- AST
- Canonical grammar

## Abstraction Retirement Rule

If an abstraction does not improve measurement, explanation, prediction, or product relevance after two evidence runs, it should be suspended.
