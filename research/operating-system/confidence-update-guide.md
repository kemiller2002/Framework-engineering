# Confidence Update Guide

Use qualitative confidence:

- Very Low
- Low
- Low-Moderate
- Moderate
- Moderate-High
- High

## Rules

- Do not use High for internal-only evidence.
- Increase confidence only when evidence survives a meaningful test.
- Decrease confidence when predictions fail or competing explanations become stronger.
- Split hypotheses instead of stretching them.
- Retire hypotheses when kill conditions are met repeatedly.
