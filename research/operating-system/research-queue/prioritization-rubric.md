# Research Queue Prioritization Rubric

## Prioritize Higher When

- the experiment reduces a major uncertainty
- the experiment distinguishes between competing explanations
- the experiment affects multiple downstream hypotheses
- the experiment checks instrument reliability or bias
- the experiment is low-cost relative to expected information gain

## Prioritize Lower When

- the experiment repeats known low-information behavior
- the experiment expands theory before uncertainty is reduced
- the experiment depends on unresolved instrumentation problems
- the experiment is mostly confirmatory without adversarial value
