# Multidimensional Classification

Status: Working draft

## Purpose

Some artifacts overlap in capabilities even when their identities differ.

FE-008 therefore separates identity from capabilities rather than forcing a layered artifact-type model.

FEMS-1 is the current measurement standard for applying this model.

Identity
↓
Capabilities
↓
Composition
↓
Domain

## Rules

- Record one provisional identity.
- Record capabilities separately.
- Treat identity as dominant function, not marketing label.
- Preserve uncertainty explicitly.
- Do not use numeric scores.

## Classification Dimensions

- identity
- capabilities
- composition
- domain

## Special Handling

### Framework Grammar

Record composition only when reasoning structure exists.

### Primitive Composition

Composition includes framework grammar and primitive cognitive operations when applicable.

### Confidence

Use categorical confidence only:

- High
- Medium
- Low

## Example Interpretation

- An artifact may have one identity and multiple capabilities.
- An artifact may be primarily a notation while still supporting modeling or communication capabilities.
- An artifact may be primarily a methodology while still supporting framework-like reasoning in limited contexts.
