# Framework Engineering Roadmap

Status: Working draft

## Phase I -- Foundation

Objective:
Establish the architectural and philosophical foundations of the discipline.

## Phase II -- Proof

Objective:
Demonstrate that Framework Engineering can successfully produce a high-quality framework.

## Phase III -- Framework Taxonomy Research

Objective:
Test whether framework archetypes exist.

Activities:

- Framework Census
- Grammar Reduction
- Blind Clustering
- Archetype Naming
- Predictive Testing

Exit Criteria:

- Candidate archetypes identified from evidence
- Frameworks classified by grammar
- FE-002 supported, refined, or rejected

## Current Research Workstreams

### FE-007 -- Knowledge Artifact Taxonomy

Purpose:
Clarify the scope of Framework Engineering by defining related structured knowledge artifact types.

### FE-008 -- Knowledge Artifact Boundary Study

Purpose:
Characterize knowledge artifacts before classification and test whether provisional primary and secondary classifications improve boundary clarity.

## Phase IV -- External Validation

Objective:
Determine whether Framework Engineering remains effective outside its creators.

## Phase V -- Discipline Maturity

Objective:
Transition Framework Engineering from a project into an engineering discipline.
